# Minimization of ER Diagrams

Minimization of ER diagrams refers to reducing the number of tables/entities in an ER diagram. Excessive tables can decrease readability and make the diagram harder to understand and manage. Minimizing ER diagrams improves clarity and efficiency, often by merging tables based on cardinality.

## Why Minimize ER Diagrams?

- **Improved Readability:** Fewer tables make the diagram easier to visualize.
- **Better Understandability:** Simpler relationships are easier to comprehend and maintain.
- **Efficient Storage and Maintenance:** Reduces redundancy and complexity in the database.
- **Faster Query Processing:** An optimized structure enhances performance.
